Internship Rules & Guidelines

These rules are designed to ensure smooth functioning, fairness, and discipline during the internship. Please read the section that applies to you carefully.

Full-Time Interns

1. Attendance Rules

  • • Must mark attendance daily through the portal.
  • • Working hours: 10:00 AM – 6:30 PM.
  • • Late marking will be recorded as "Late Attendance".
  • • Absence without prior notice is recorded as Absent.

2. Leave Policy

  • • Request leave at least 1 day in advance.
  • • Uninformed absence for more than 2 days may result in termination.
  • • Medical leave requires a valid certificate.

3. Work & Conduct

  • • Complete assigned tasks within deadlines.
  • • Maintain professional behavior at all times.
  • • Use company resources responsibly.

4. Reporting

  • • Submit weekly reports to assigned HR/mentor.
  • • Report technical or operational issues immediately.

5. Disciplinary Actions

  • • First violation → warning notice.
  • • Repeated violations → possible stipend/certificate impact.
  • • Severe misconduct → removal from internship program.

Part-Time Interns

1. Attendance Rules

  • • Mark attendance on working days only.
  • • Working hours: 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM.
  • • Late attendance must be reported to HR.

2. Leave Policy

  • • Inform HR at least 24 hours in advance for any leave.
  • • Consistent absenteeism may affect continuation in the program.

3. Work & Conduct

  • • Complete tasks assigned for part-time hours.
  • • Maintain professionalism and follow communication guidelines.
  • • Avoid using company resources for personal purposes.

4. Reporting

  • • Submit updates daily/weekly as instructed by HR.
  • • Notify HR promptly of issues or blockers.

5. Disciplinary Actions

  • • Violation of attendance or work policies → warning notice.
  • • Repeated violations → removal from internship program.

By continuing to use this system, you agree to follow the above rules and guidelines applicable to your internship type.
